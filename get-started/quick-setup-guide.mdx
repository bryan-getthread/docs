Ready to kick off your team's service collaboration journey? Setting up Thread is simple! Follow this guide to complete the first-time setup process, starting with creating your Thread Workspace.

In this guide, you will:

1. Create your Thread Workspace.
2. Setup your ticketing system.
3. Design your customer chat experience.
4. Select your default service settings.
5. Configure Thread Inbox to receive chats.
6. Start learning Inbox with your first chat.

## **1. Create Your Thread Workspace**

<Warning>
  You only need one workspace per company, so decide who will complete this step.
</Warning>

1. Navigate to the Thread Workspace **setup link** provided by your Customer Success Manager, or as outlined in your **Thread Onboarding Plan**.
2. Enter your email and click **Submit**.
  ![](/images/f72d39a9-2023-08-29-13-57-43.png)
3. Enter the one-time passcode sent to your email and click **Submit**.
4. Enter your **Full name**, **Workspace name** and **Upload your logo**.If you don't have your company logo ready, you can use the default "Thread" logo for now. You can easily change it later from the Thread Admin Panel once your workspace is set up.
  ![](/images/1b4093c6-2023-08-29-14-01-06.png)
5. Once you're finished, click **Continue**!

## **3. Design Your Customer Chat Experience**

With your ticketing system integrated, it's time to personalize the chat support experience for your customers. Add your branding to make the chat experience truly yours.

<Note>
  Don't have all the assets you need?
  
  No worries, you can click "
  
  Continue
  
  " and complete this setup later. We recommend setting up what you can, even if it's not perfect—like the Primary Color and Bot Name. You can also use one of our default Avatars.
</Note>

![](/images/6023c45c-onboarding-1-sign-up-2.png)

1. Set your **Primary Color**. Use one of our pre-defined colors, or choose a color from the color wheel by clicking the **+**.
2. **Give your bot an avatar**. Use a pre-built avatar or upload your own by clicking the **+**.
3. Lastly, **give your bot a name**! We recommend something fun that your customers will recognize.
4. Click **Continue** when done with this step!

## **4. Select Your Default Service Settings**

Next, we'll set up default settings for incoming chats. The options vary slightly depending on your ticketing system.

<Tip>
  You can change these settings later. If your team wants to route chats for different customers to different service boards or queues, you can override these settings later in the Thread Client settings.
</Tip>

### **Chat Settings for ConnectWise PSA**

![](/images/6ec69dd8-onboarding-1-sign-up-3.png)

1. **Service Board** - Set the default service board you want chats to route in to.
2. **Email Source** - Set the source you use in ConnectWise to categorize email tickets
3. **Phone Source** - Set the source you use in ConnectWise to categorize phone tickets

### **Chat Settings for Autotask PSA**

![](/images/b9fffe31-onboarding-1-sign-up-4.png)

1. **Service Board** - Set the default service board you want chats to route in to.
2. **Default Status** - Set the default status for incoming chats
3. **Ticket Types** - Set the Default ticket type for incoming chats
4. **Chat Source** - Set the default source you use in Autotask to categorize chats
5. **Email Source** - Set the source you use in ConnectWise to categorize email tickets
6. **Phone Source** - Set the source you use in ConnectWise to categorize phone tickets

### **Chat Settings for HaloPSA**

1. **Team** - Set the default team you want chats to route in to.
2. **Default Ticket Type** - Set the Default ticket type for incoming chats.
3. **Action for sending customer-facing notes** - Choose the action in HaloPSA to send customer-facing (public) notes.
4. **Action for emailing contacts** - Choose the action in HaloPSA to send emails to users.
5. **Action for sending internal "private notes"** - Choose the action in HaloPSA for sending internal (private) notes.
  ![](/images/3f5f3fb4-image.png)

## **5. Get Started with Thread Inbox**

It's time to set up Inbox! Inbox is where your Service Team works to respond to customers in real-time! It is the app you use, as the service provider, to collaborate with your teammates and customers. Inbox was designed to give your team service superpowers so that you can focus on providing a great customer experience!

<Note>
  Inbox isn’t just for managing customer chats! It can also be used across your organization to manage all service tickets seamlessly integrated with your ticketing system.
</Note>

When you log into Thread Inbox for the first time, you'll notice the "**Views**" section on the left-hand side. Let’s walk through the steps to create your very first view!

#### **Steps to Create a New View**

1. Click the "**+**" icon next to Views in the left-hand navigation
2. Apply **"Source" is not blank**. Alternatively, you can apply additional filters, such as Service Boards, Teams, or Queues, to help organize and display all tickets more effectively.
3. Enter a name for your view **All Tickets**
4. Customize Your View (Optional)
5. (Optional) Choose an emoji to represent your view
6. (Optional) Invite members to access the view
7. Click **Save**

#### **Learn More About Views & Insights**

To explore more about creating and managing views, check out our [guide on](/inbox/views-and-insights) [Views & Insights](/inbox/views-and-insights)
