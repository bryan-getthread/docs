## **Get Started with Intents in Minutes**

The quickest way to begin automating your support workflows is by using **Thread’s ready-made intent templates**. These templates are designed to cover the most common service requests, helping you handle up to **50% of your service volume**—right out of the box.

Let’s walk you through how to set them up and customize them for your needs.

### **1. Start by Adding Recommended Intents**

We've analyzed thousands of service tickets to uncover the most frequent request categories. These high-impact intent templates are available directly in your **Thread Admin panel**.

You can:

1. **Add templates individually** – Browse, customize, and activate as needed.
2. **Add all templates at once** – Instantly populate your entire catalog.

<Note>
  When using “
  
  Add all templates
  
  ", all intents will appear as Inactive in your catalog. This gives you time to review and fine-tune them before activating them for your clients.
</Note>

### **2. Have a Custom Use Case? Ask Triage Agent!**

Got a specific intent in mind, but don’t know how to build it? Let **Triage Agent** do the heavy lifting.

Yes—you can literally **use an intent to create intents**. Just start a conversation with our support team and ask for help crafting your next one.

![](/images/4942d29f-inception.gif)

#### **Try It Out**

1. Go to [getthread.com/help](https://getthread.com/help)
2. Start a **support chat**
3. Ask for help building a custom intent

**Example Request:**

> *“I’m an IT managed service provider. I manage backups for client workstations, servers, OneDrive, etc. I need help creating an intent for data retrieval requests. Here are examples my clients often send…”*

The Triage Agent will help you build, refine, and finalize the perfect intent based on your real-world needs.

Once you're satisfied with the output, head to:

> **Thread Admin → Magic Agents → Catalog → Create Intent** and paste in the content generated by Triage Agent, and you're good to go!
