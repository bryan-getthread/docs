## Microsoft Teams – Enabling Pay-As-You-Go Calling for Resource Accounts

<Note>
  ⚠️ Important: Microsoft Licensing Change – Effective November 1, 2025
  
  Microsoft has announced that starting
  
  November 1, 2025
  
  , Resource Accounts (such as Auto Attendants and Call Queues)
  
  will no longer be able to make outbound calls
  
  without activating
  
  Communication Credits
  
  .
  
  To activate Communication Credits, you need at least
  
  1 Teams Phone Pay-As-You-Go Calling Plan
  
  license on the tenant.
  
  ⚠️
  
  Note:
  
  Direct Routing numbers are
  
  not
  
  affected by this licensing change. This only applies to Calling Plan customers.
</Note>

<Tip>
  📌
  
  You do NOT need a license for every user.
  
  Microsoft requires just
  
  1 license per tenant
  
  to enable this functionality.
</Tip>

**Official Microsoft Reference:** [Teams Phone Extensibility – Outbound Call Prerequisites](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/azure/communication-services/concepts/interop/tpe/teams-phone-extensibility-connectivity-cost#outbound-call-prerequisites)

**Who Is Affected?**

- Tenants using **Microsoft Calling Plans** assigned to Teams Resource Accounts (Auto Attendants, Call Queues)
- Any Resource Account making **outbound PSTN calls** after November 1, 2025
- **Not affected:** Tenants using Direct Routing or Operator Connect (check with your carrier for Operator Connect)

**What You Need to Do**

Purchase **1 license** of **Teams Phone with Pay-As-You-Go Calling** for the tenant, then enable Pay-As-You-Go billing on that subscription.

There are two plans available:

- **Zone 1 – US** (for domestic US customers)
- **Zone 2 – International** (for international customers)

### How To

#### Step 1: Purchase the License

1. Log in to the **Microsoft 365 Admin Center** at [admin.microsoft.com](https://admin.microsoft.com).
2. In the left navigation, go to **Billing &gt; Purchase services** (or **Marketplace &gt; All Products**).
3. Search for **"Teams Phone with pay-as-you-go calling"**.
4. Select the appropriate zone:
  - **Country Zone 1 – US** for US-based tenants
  - **Country Zone 2** for international tenants
5. Purchase **1 license** (you only need one for the entire tenant).

#### Step 2: Open the Subscription Details

1. In the **Microsoft 365 Admin Center**, go to **Billing &gt; Your products**.
2. Locate **Teams Phone with pay-as-you-go calling (country zone 1 – US)** (or your applicable zone).
3. Click **Details** to open the subscription management page.

![](/images/bf293701-xAfa9wu0)

#### Step 3: Enable Pay-As-You-Go Services

1. Scroll down on the subscription details page to find the section titled **"Pay-as-you-go services"**.
2. You will see a table listing:
  - **Product name:** Phone services consumption
  - **Status:** Off (or On)
3. If Status shows **"Off"** — click on it to toggle it **On**.
4. Confirm the change when prompted.

![](/images/691e6190-79BNJMXu)

#### Step 4: Verify & Monitor

- Once enabled, the **Status** column should show **"On"** for Phone services consumption.
- You can click **"Manage costs"** to set a spending limit or review usage.
- Use **"See Teams call history for your organization"** to monitor call activity.

<Note>
  ⏱️ Propagation Time
  
  Please allow 12–24 hours
  
  for this change to fully propagate across the tenant. Resource Account outbound calling may not work immediately after enabling — this is expected.
</Note>
