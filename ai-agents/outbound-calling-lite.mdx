Outbound calling lets your technicians place calls to customers directly from the
Inbox, with a verified business caller ID shown no matter which device or number
the tech actually dials from.

### Why it matters

- **Consistent business identity** — every call your team places shows your
  verified caller ID, not a personal cell or random extension.
- **Recorded and transcribed automatically** — each outbound call lands in the
  ticket as a transcript, available from both the Admin dashboard and the Inbox
  sidebar.
- **Works with any phone setup** — direct numbers, extensions under a DID, or
  personal devices. Customers always see your main verified number.
- **Supercharged by Super Magic** — query the live transcript mid-call to pull up
  docs, then have Super Magic summarize the call and draft a Time Pad entry the
  moment you hang up.

### How it works

1. An **admin** adds an outbound agent and verifies the caller ID number(s)
   customers will see.
2. A technician places a call from the ticket; Thread connects the tech's device
   and presents the verified caller ID to the customer.
3. The call is recorded, transcribed, and attached to the ticket automatically.
