Connect Notion and Super Magic can **read and write your pages, databases, and docs** — pull up the runbook mid-ticket, or turn a hard-won fix into documentation without leaving the conversation.

### What you can ask

> "Find the client onboarding runbook in Notion"

> "What does our Notion say about VPN setup for Acme?"

> "Turn this ticket's resolution into a runbook page in our Notion database"

> "Add what we learned today to the known-issues page"

Creating or editing Notion content goes through Super Magic's **Confirm action** card first.

#### Set it up

Notion is a **connector**: an admin makes it available, then **each member signs in with their own Notion account** — Super Magic sees exactly what you can see in Notion, nothing more.

**Admin (once):**

1. Open **Super Magic settings → Connectors**.
2. Turn on the **Notion** toggle.

**Each member (once):**

1. Open the **Connectors** page in your Inbox.
2. Click **Connect** on the Notion row, sign in, and choose what to share when Notion asks.
3. The row shows **Signed in as** your account — done.

#### Troubleshooting

- **Notion not on your Connectors page?** Your admin hasn't enabled it, or your workspace isn't in the connectors release yet.
- **Super Magic can't find a page you know exists?** Your Notion connection only covers what you shared during sign-in — reconnect and grant access to the right spaces.
- Anything else: [**Super Magic Admin Guide → Troubleshooting**](/super-magic/super-magic-admin-guide-setup-access-safety).

#### Available tools

Notion's official MCP server (`mcp.notion.com` — the same endpoint Thread connects to) currently exposes **16 tools**. Verified June 10, 2026 against the live server:

| Area | Tools |
| --- | --- |
| Find & read | notion-search , notion-fetch |
| Create & edit pages | notion-create-pages , notion-update-page , notion-move-pages , notion-duplicate-page |
| Databases & views | notion-create-database , notion-update-data-source , notion-create-view , notion-update-view , notion-query-database-view |
| Meeting notes | notion-query-meeting-notes |
| Comments | notion-create-comment , notion-get-comments |
| Workspace info | notion-get-users , notion-get-teams |

> <Note>
>   Notes: the set is controlled by Notion and can change — Super Magic picks up changes automatically (tool list refreshes every ~5 minutes) and registers the vendor's tools
>   
>   1:1, nothing filtered
>   
>   . In Super Magic each tool appears prefixed with the connector slug (e.g.
>   
>   notion_notion-search
>   
>   ). Access covers only what you shared with the connection during sign-in.
> </Note>
