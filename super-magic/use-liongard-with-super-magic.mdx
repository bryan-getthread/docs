Connect Liongard and Super Magic can **answer questions about your clients' IT
infrastructure** — surfacing the inventory, configurations, and change history
Liongard tracks, without leaving the ticket conversation.

### What you can ask

> "What does Liongard show for Acme's domain and SSL expiration?"

> "List Acme's Microsoft 365 admins from Liongard"

> "Has anything changed on Acme's firewall config recently?"

Liongard is read-only context for Super Magic — it brings infrastructure detail
*into* the conversation to speed up diagnosis.

### Set it up

Liongard is a **connector**: an admin makes it available, then **each member signs
in with their own Liongard account** — Super Magic sees exactly what you can see
in Liongard, nothing more.

**Admin (once):**

1. Open **Super Magic settings → Connectors**.
2. Turn on the **Liongard** toggle. (This makes it available — it doesn't connect
   anyone yet.)

**Each member (once):**

1. Open the **Connectors** page in your Inbox.
2. Click **Connect** on the Liongard row and sign in.
3. The row shows **Signed in as** your account — you're ready.

### Troubleshooting

- **Liongard not on your Connectors page?** Your admin hasn't enabled it, or your
  workspace isn't in the connectors release yet.
- **Super Magic can't find a system you expect?** Your connection only covers what
  your Liongard account can access — reconnect and confirm the right environments
  are in scope.
- Anything else: [**Super Magic Admin Guide → Troubleshooting**](/super-magic/super-magic-admin-guide-setup-access-safety).

<Note>
  The tools available through this connector are provided by Liongard and may
  change; Super Magic picks up changes automatically. Each member's access
  reflects their own Liongard sign-in.
</Note>
