Magic Analytics lets you take your data with you — download it, get alerted when something changes, or have a dashboard delivered to your inbox on a schedule. You can act on a **whole dashboard** or a **single tile/query**.

## Tile and dashboard actions

You can download an individual tile from its **⋮** menu. To download the full dashboard, use the download option in the bottom-left corner of the dashboard.

| What it does |  |
| --- | --- |
| Ask a follow up question | Opens the Dashboard Agent focused on that tile so you can dig deeper (AI Pro). See Dashboard Agent . |
| Download… | Export that tile's data or image. |
| Alert… | Get notified when the tile's results meet a condition you set. |
| Schedule delivery… | Have that tile delivered automatically on a recurring schedule. |

The menu also shows how many **filters are applied** and the **"Query ran…"** timestamp so you know how current the data is.

## Downloading

You can download an individual tile from its **⋮** menu. To download the full dashboard, use the download option in the bottom-left corner of the dashboard.

1. **PDF** — great for sharing a polished snapshot; options for paper size, single-column layout, and expanding tables.
2. **PNG** — an image of the dashboard or tile.
3. **XLSX / CSV** — the underlying data for your own analysis (a CSV download packages each tile as its own file).

## Alerts

Alerts can be created from an individual tile using its **⋮** menu or for an entire dashboard from the options in the bottom-left corner. Alerts notify you only when a specified condition is met, such as when results change or new results are returned, so you can focus on exceptions instead of checking dashboards manually.

## Scheduled delivery

Schedules deliver content **on a recurring frequency** (daily, weekly, or a custom cadence) to a destination such as **email**. Pick the format (PDF, PNG, XLSX, CSV), the timezone, and which filters to apply. You can schedule from an individual tile using its **⋮** menu or for an entire dashboard from the options in the bottom-left corner.

<Note>
  Because data refreshes daily, scheduling a morning delivery gives you yesterday's complete numbers.
</Note>

## FAQ

Why doesn't my export match what I see on screen?

Confirm the filters and date range were the same, and check the "Query ran…" timestamp — the data refreshes daily.
