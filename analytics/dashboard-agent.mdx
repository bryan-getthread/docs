## What it is

The Dashboard Agent is Magic Analytics' built-in AI assistant for asking questions **right on your dashboards**. Ask in plain English and it answers — often with a chart or table — drawing on the same governed topics that power your dashboards. No query building required, and it only ever has access to **your organization's data**.

<Note>
  The Dashboard Agent is available to
  
  AI Pro
  
  members.
</Note>

## What it can do

- **Ask follow-up questions** about any tile in natural language
- **Drill into a subset** of the data and re-aggregate the results
- **Summarize** what a chart is showing
- **Focus on a specific chart** — click it, or choose **Ask a follow up question** from the tile's **⋮** menu
- Go **beyond what's on the dashboard** by exploring the available topics

### How to open it

Two ways:

1. Open the **Dashboard Agent** panel on a published dashboard and start typing.
2. On any tile, click the **⋮** (three dots) → **Ask a follow up question** — this opens the agent already focused on that tile.

The agent stays focused on the chart you selected until you clear the focus or close the panel.

### Great questions to ask

1. *"How many tickets did the Triage Agent handle last month?"*
2. *"What's my Voice AI call volume week over week?"*
3. *"Which customers don't have Messenger enabled yet?"*
4. *"Where are auto-categorization exceptions highest, and how do I improve accuracy?"*
5. *"What's my estimated time saved this quarter?"*

### Tips for great answers

| Tip | Why it helps |
| --- | --- |
| Name the area | Mentioning a topic (Triage Agent, Voice AI, Messenger…) helps it scope the question. |
| Give a time frame | "last 30 days", "Q2", "week over week" produces sharper results. |
| Ask one thing at a time | Break multi-part questions into steps. |
| Iterate | Refine if the first answer isn't quite right ("now break that out by customer"). |

### Good to know

1. Answers come from the **daily refresh** — the Dashboard Agent isn't real-time.
2. It can only answer within the **topics** you have access to (see [*Topics you can explore*](/analytics/topics-you-can-explore)).
3. Some topics are **pre-aggregated**, so it can summarize and re-cut those metrics but can't drill to individual rows (see [*Things to know & gotchas*](/analytics/things-to-know-and-gotchas)).

### FAQ

Who can use it?

AI Pro members.

Is my data private?

Yes — it can only access your own organization's data.

Why might it not answer a question?

It may be outside the available topics, or need to be phrased more specifically. Try naming the topic and a time frame.
