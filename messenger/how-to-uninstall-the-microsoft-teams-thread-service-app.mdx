---
title: "How to Uninstall the Microsoft Teams Service App"
description: "Remove the Thread Microsoft Teams Service App from a customer tenant by uninstalling in Thread Admin and deleting it from the Teams Admin Center Manage Apps."
---

If you wish to uninstall this app, you can follow these steps:

1. In [Thread Admin](https://admin.getthread.com/dashboard/clients/companies), find the company that is linked to the Teams App that you would like to uninstall and click on Uninstall
  ![](/images/203db797-image.png)
2. Navigate to the **Microsoft Teams Admin Portal** and click **Teams Apps** -&gt; [Manage Apps](https://admin.teams.microsoft.com/policies/manage-apps)
  ![](/images/464ece5c-image.png)
3. Search for the name of the Service App in the search bar
  ![](/images/9af7eced-image.png)
4. Click on the app and then on the right side of the screen click "Actions" -&gt; "Delete"
  ![](/images/473fe868-image.png)

And that's it!
