### **Show all tickets in Chat (formerly "Proactive Messages") option**

- **IMPORTANT: Applies to all tickets** **created in PSA**
- **IMPORTANT: These options can be overwritten on client company settings level**

|  | Existing tickets: option is enabled | Existing tickets: option is disabled | New tickets: option is enabled | New tickets: option is disabled |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Messenger (list) | will be visible | will not be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |
| Teams Support Tab (Messenger list) | will be visible | will not be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |
| Teams Chat Tab | will arrive on update | will continue to be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |
| Teams Group Chats | will arrive on update | will continue to be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |
| Slack Home Tab (list) | will be visible | will not be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |
| Slack Message Tab | will arrive on update | will continue to be visible | will be visible | will not be visible |

### **Excluded specific Boards/Queues or Exclude specific sources options**

- **IMPORTANT: Applies to all tickets**
- **IMPORTANT: These options can be overwritten on client company settings level**

|  | Existing tickets: if ticket did not match then excluded board (queue) or source is changed and now matches | Existing tickets: if ticket did match then excluded board (queue) or source is changed and now does not match | New tickets: tickets do not match any of excluded boards (queues) or sources | New tickets: tickets match one of excluded boards (queues) or sources | Existing tickets: if ticket did not match then board (queue) or source is changed on the ticket and now matches | Existing tickets: if ticket did match then board (queue) or source is changed on the ticket and now does not match |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Messenger (list) | will not be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will not be visible | will be visible |
| Teams Support Tab (Messenger list) | will not be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will not be visible | will be visible |
| Teams Chat Tab | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update |
| Teams Group Chats | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update |
| Slack Home Tab (list) | will not be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will not be visible | will be visible |
| Slack Message Tab | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update | will be visible | will not be visible | will continue to be visible | will arrive on update |
