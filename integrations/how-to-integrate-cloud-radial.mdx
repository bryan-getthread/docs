<Note>
  If you already integrated CloudRadial, please ensure that you delete the header text before performing the following.
</Note>

Thread enables your service team to chat with clients in Microsoft Teams and Slack, plus a whole lot more. To integrate CloudRadial, please follow these steps:

1. Navigate to Thread Admin Panel -&gt; [Integrations](https://admin.getthread.com/dashboard/integrations)
2. Click to setup your Cloudradial Integration
3. Enter your CloudRadial portal URL -&gt; Click **Connect** & **Save**
  ![](/images/cc5d3ef4-2023-12-15-11-07-25.png)
4. Retrieve your AppID [here](https://admin.getthread.com/dashboard/messenger/settings/installation) (keep this AppID handy, you will need it shortly)
5. Next, Navigate to the home page of CloudRadial application.
6. Go to Partners -&gt; Settings.
  ![](/images/d5b4c4e8-image.png)
7. Then, click Integrations on the right side.
  ![](/images/fd55e344-image.png)
8. On the right side, click thread integration.
9. Click enable thread, enter the AppID, and click submit.
  ![](/images/a1968144-image.png)

And that is it!

### How to disable the CloudRadial Integration

To disable the CloudRadial Integration, navigate to the home page of the CloudRadial application, and then go to Partners -&gt; Settings -&gt; Integrations (same steps as above)

Then you can uncheck the "Enable Thread?" box.

![](/images/ef29abcc-image.png)

Then hit submit and the integration will be disabled.
